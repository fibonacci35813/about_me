Selected Projects

ICUAS’22 – Drone Projectile Control

Designed a trajectory and tuned a PID controller for a drone to launch a ball that accurately hit an AR tag target. Our team finished 6th among 53 international teams at the International Conference of Unmanned Aircraft Systems 2022.

Autonomous Driving on F‑1/10 Vehicle

Developed algorithms for lane tracking, obstacle detection and autonomous decision‑making using LiDAR and camera data. Implemented image processing (Hough transforms) to generate waypoints and compared performance with CNN‑based controllers.

Deep Learning for RUL Estimation

Worked with the University of Birmingham to tune hyperparameters using the Bees Algorithm for estimating the Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of bearings. Achieved results outperforming the IEEE PHM 2012 competition winner.

Bees Algorithm for UAV Path Planning

Utilised a two‑parameter version of the Bees Algorithm to optimise UAV paths. Compared the method to wolf optimisers and obtained superior obstacle avoidance and efficiency.
